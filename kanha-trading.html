Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Do you provide GST invoices?
Yes. Kanha Trading is a GST registered business (GSTIN: 06BGUPY6724N1Z8). Proper tax invoices are issued for every B2B and commercial purchase, eligible for input tax credit.
What is the minimum order quantity?
There is no strict MOQ for most catalog items. However, special pricing and volume discounts kick in for bulk orders. Contact us for a customized quote based on your requirement.
Do you deliver outside Faridabad?
Yes. We dispatch pan-India through reliable logistics partners. Same-day dispatch is available within Delhi NCR for in-stock items. Outstation delivery typically takes 2–5 business days.
Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?
For most products, samples are available at standard pricing. Drop us an inquiry with the product specification and quantity needed, and our team will guide you on sample availability.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), UPI, cheque, and cash. For first-time large orders, advance payment is required. Regular B2B customers may avail credit terms on approval.
Do you supply only specific brands?
We stock both branded and unbranded industrial-grade products. Tell us your preferred brand or quality level, and we'll match the right specification at the best price point.
